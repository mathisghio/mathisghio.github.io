About

I am Mathis Ghio, a 23-year-old professional wingfoil and sailing athlete with a lifelong passion for watersports. I am currently completing my 4th year at INSA Lyon, specializing in Materials for Sports Studies.

Registered as a high-level athlete on the French Ministry’s elite lists from 2017 to 2026, I have been competing internationally since the age of seven. I began sailing at AVCR (Association Voile Carry-le-Rouet) and have consistently demonstrated dedication, discipline, and excellence.

Today, I am under a professional contract and contribute to the R&D teams of leading brands such as Ozone, Levitaz Hydrofoils, and Forward Wip. My weekly training regimen includes four on-water sessions and three indoor/outdoor physical preparation sessions, ensuring peak performance in competitions and technical innovation.

Driven by performance, innovation, and a passion for the sport, I strive to push the limits of wingfoiling while sharing my expertise and promoting watersports globally.
