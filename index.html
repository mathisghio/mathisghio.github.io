About

French professional wingfoil and sailing athlete, competing internationally since childhood. My career is built around high-performance racing, technical innovation and long-term consistency at elite level.

Member of the French Elite Athlete list (2017–2026), I combine international competition with engineering studies at INSA Lyon, specializing in materials science within a sport-performance curriculum.

Beyond racing, I actively collaborate in R&D programs with leading brands to push equipment performance, reliability and speed.
